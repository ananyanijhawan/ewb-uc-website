OUR PROJECTS

Engineers Without Borders is involved in a variety of projects across the world. Our chapter at University of Cincinnati has projects in Kenya and Tanzania, as well as local projects in Cincinnati.

2006 - 2009

Otho Abwao

Started in 2006, Otho Abwao, Kenya was our chapter's first partner community. This community of 500 people identified the need for clean and sanitary water that was easy to access.

As part of our joint venture with Village Life Outreach Project, we are working to provide a year-round source of potable water to a the community of Nyambogo, a village of roughly 5,000 people.
