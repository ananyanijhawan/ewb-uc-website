ABOUT ME

A BIT ABOUT YOURS TRULY

I AM A SOPHOMORE AT THE University of Cincinnati majoring in Computer Science. I love coding, designing, and traveling in my free time. From watching a Turtle draw a rectangle 13 years ago to dynamically generating data in a website today, I have learned a lot with my interaction with computers — a relationship I have come to appreciate over the years. Programming provides me endless opportunities to reinvent myself. Every problem presents with itself a unique learning challenge.

I am really passionate about programming and innovation. I aspire to create beautiful and useful technology that flushes out complexities in our daily lives and channels us to focus on what we love doing. I am inspired by amazing services like GitHub, Bootstrap, and Amazon Alexa among others and hope to build something like these one day. What really inspire me, though, are copy-paste and undo-redo shortcuts — humans are lazy and fallible after all.
